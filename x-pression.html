CONCEPT, EDITING AND DESIGN
Rodrigo Amâncio, Maria Proença & Duarte Costa
X-PRESSION is a magazine that brings together a wide range of voices. It features a group of artists whose ideas challenge the status quo and question the norms that determine whether or not their identity is accepted by society. The first edition, Where Worlds Collide, is dedicated to music and to ideas that, on the one hand, act as safe spaces, and on the other, force us to reflect on the state of the ‘world outside’.

The interplay between the texts and images seeks to immerse us in the 4 fictional worlds of this issue (Biophilia, Xenomorph, Wanderlust and Anthropogenic), in a journey guided by their opening texts.

As a complement, the posters contain a code leading to a playlist of the artists’ music. As well as helping us immerse ourselves in the fictional worlds, this also reflects the message that each artist conveys through their various art forms.
