CONCEPT, WRITING, DESIGN AND EXHIBITION
Rodrigo Amâncio, Maria Proença, Francisco Janes & Duarte Costa
For this project, my teammates and I were asked to create a fictional story based on a film and to communicate it in a project room. Therefore, METAGENESIS creates a science fiction-like narrative about the birth of a new entity that goes beyond the biological conditions inherent to human beings. Using a non-linear narrative, it creates a meditation on gender and identity. The possibility of the existence of bodies without a fixed form, which will question the political and social scenario, is based on Titane, a film by Julia Ducournau.

In the room, the sound, heat and red light simulated the inside of a womb. After the array of images on the 4 screens, the room was dominated by the main video. And a voice brought the fiction to life, an intervention by a new being who addressed the observer in a propagandistic tone. With the fluctuations of light and the lack of contours of the dark room, the goal was to create a sensory suspension and an immersive experience.

The project involved not only the experience of the project room itself (installation, sound and videos), but also multiple communication objects: poster, invitation, room flyers, book (that documented the whole process), teaser, social media and website (to gather all the recordings).
