CONCEPT, RESEARCH AND DESIGN
Rodrigo Amâncio
SIMULACRUM is an editorial project focused on digital imagery. From documentaries to manipulated images, it explores in an essayistic way how the latter can produce unreality and blur the boundaries between fact and fiction. The project consists of a book and a website and was conceived as an existential approach to our perception of what is real and to our emotional limits. The aim of the project was to seek possible research strategies in design, in this case guiding theory through practice.

The book is an anthology and seeks to create parallel readings between the various essays and cross-reference them with projects in design, cinema and virtual reality. The texts are constantly interrupted by images that either appear in the margins or occupy entire spreads to create longer breaks. The website points to the dissemination of digital content. It focuses on the projects, expands their content and intersects them with different essays.
