CONCEPT, EDITING AND DESIGN
Rodrigo Amâncio, Maria Proença & Duarte Costa
Deviant is a magazine that seeks a refuge for self-expression, departing from the experience in a significant space. The first edition, The Night As a Place, is dedicated to clubbing, identity, music, and to how, in the late 20th century, the nightclubs served as safe spaces, escapes from a world that does not allow some people to take on their own identity without restriction or fear.

The images and texts seek to simulate this experience, guided by the descriptions that separate the chapters (covering New York, London, Paris and Berlin). Moreover, in the magazine’s presentation video, the images, rhythmic soundtrack and introspective voiceover aim to stimulate the concept of the printed edition, an introduction to the created space.
