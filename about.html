ABOUT ME
PROFILE
(b. 2001 / based in Lisbon, Portugal)
Olá! I’m Rodrigo, a graphic designer specializing in editorial, branding and web design. My recent projects and investigation have allowed me to explore a deep interest in the values of imagination, critics and situated knowledge.

I’m focused on creating experiences that allow me to exert my interest in arts, literature and interdisciplinarity. Always curious about learning something new and, overall, I define myself as a creative person, deeply passionate about exploring how design can tell a story.
